### How it works
1. Upload PDF
2. Select pages to delete
3. Download result

### Limits / usage
- Free: up to 20 MB and up to 200 pages
- Max: up to 100 MB and up to 500 pages
- Password-protected or encrypted PDFs are not supported
- Credits may be required for heavier jobs
